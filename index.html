Farm to Table

Our Dining

Start your morning with a full buffet breakfast on our garden terrace, and end your day with cuisine that blends Kenya's rich culinary heritage with international flavours — all prepared by our passionate in-house chefs.

Kenyan Kitchen ▾

Authentic nyama choma, ugali, sukuma wiki, and coastal Swahili dishes prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. A true taste of home.

Continental Breakfast ▾

A generous spread of pastries, fresh tropical fruit, eggs cooked to order, premium coffees, and fresh-squeezed juices — included in every room rate.

International Cuisine ▾

From Italian pasta to Indian curry and Asian stir-fry, our diverse evening menu caters to every palate with expertly prepared dishes and fine wine pairings.

Poolside Snacks & Cocktails ▾

Refresh by the pool with grilled skewers, artisan sandwiches, tropical smoothies, and our signature Savanna Sundowner cocktail — the perfect end to an African afternoon.
